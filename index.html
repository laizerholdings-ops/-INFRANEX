Our Specialties

Fibreglass Pools

We offer high-grade UV-resistant fibreglass installations that are non-porous and easy to maintain. Our process ensures a quick turnaround without compromising structural integrity, available in various modern shapes and vibrant color finishes.

Marbelite Finish

For a classic, premium aesthetic, our Marbelite hand-plastered finishes provide a smooth, durable, and slip-resistant surface. This traditional cement-based finish is perfect for custom-shaped pools and offers unmatched longevity in South African climates.

Total Renovations

Breathe new life into aging pools. Our renovation services cover everything from coping stone replacement and mosaic tiling to pump room modernization and structural reshaping, ensuring your investment regains its original splendor.

Leak & Repairs

Using advanced pressure testing and dye detection, we locate and repair leaks with precision. We specialize in structural crack injection and re-lining, preventing water loss and protecting your property's foundation from erosion.
